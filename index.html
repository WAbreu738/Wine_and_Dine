Using Wine & Dine

Wine & Dine is an application to treat food and libation boredom

Our web application offers exciting features that enable you to input food preferences and get a list of recipe and wine results based on those interests and parameters.

What are you in the mood for? You will be prompted with questions about the type of food you want to eat. Input the main meal item you are interested in (whether it be chicken, beef, or fish) along with some side ingredients (vegetables, starches, etc.). Just click the button to get started!

Cocktail & Mixed Drink Recipes

Feeling something different or stronger? Try a cocktail with your meal instead of a wine

Not feeling wine with dinner? We will help you come up with some other options instead. Click the button to generate a cocktail or mixed drink to go with your meal!

Featured Recipe

Filet Mignon With Cognac Sauce

This elegant dish features tender filet mignon steak served with a rich and flavorful cognac sauce. It's perfect for a special occasion or a romantic dinner.

Ingredients:

  • 4 filet mignon steaks
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 shallots, finely chopped
  • 1/2 cup cognac
  • 1 cup beef broth
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Featured Drink Pairing

Cabernet Sauvignon

Cabernet Sauvignon is a bold and robust red wine known for its rich flavors and full body. It's one of the most popular red wine varietals and pairs well with a variety of dishes.

Cabernet Sauvignon pairs particularly well with rich and hearty dishes such as steak, lamb, and aged cheeses. Its bold flavors and firm tannins complement the savory flavors of these foods.
